Is International shipping available?

Yes, shipping across the globe is available and to most countries, we ship the products free of cost.

  • The customs charges must be taken by you.
  • If in-case of return/refund, you will have to take care of shipping charges and other service costs.
  • Minimum weight eligible for Free international shipping is 25kg.
  • Due to overload of orders, the delivery might take more than 50 days.

Lost my warranty, am I still eligible to claim?

If you have the purchase details along with date of purchase, and if the current date is inside the warranty time, then you are eligible to claim your warranty.
